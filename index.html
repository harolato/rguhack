Welcome to the RGUHack realm!

Travel up to “The North” to participate in the first RGU Hackathon. You will be able to get hands on some neat Hardware and APIs lined up from our sponsors for you to hack on. 24 hours of restless hacking to bring your ideas and hacks to life.

Count me in!

24 Hours

From 10am on Saturday to 5pm on Sunday, hackers will be racing with time to bring their hacks to life. In the meantime we will be providing them with FREE food, drinks and entertainment.

90+ Hackers

There will be over 90 most talented students in the country competing against each other to come up with the most impressive and revolutionary project. You can become one of them!

Beginner Friendly

It is absolutely no problem if you have never attended a hackathon before. You will most definitely not be the only one there. The main purpose of this hackathon is to learn something new, have fun and see how other people work. You don’t even have to be a computing student to come and contribute.

FAQ



What is RGUHack?

RGUHack is an all-inclusive, free event organised for students by students. You will create teams with like minded people with a common aim; to create something awesome in just 24 hours.

What is MLH?

MLH or Major League Hacking is the official student hackathon league. With their help we are able to organise this event and bring you some cool pieces of hardware, that you will be able to play with during the event.

Do I have to pay anything?

Absolutely not. This event is completely free for you, including food, drink, snacks, wifi and some amazing prizes for the winners.

What do I need to bring?

You will definitely need your laptop and charger. It is also advised to bring your phone charger and any other cables you think you will need. Bring some warm and comfortable clothes as the hackathon is quite long. Toiletries are important too! Nothing feels better than changing into fresh set of clothes before the final presentations.

Do I need to have a team before the event?

You are welcome to organise your team beforehand but don’t worry if you don’t have one. At the start of the event there will be time to find yourself a team and we will be there to help you out if you are struggling.

Can I prepare my hack before the event?

Sorry but no. If you want to be eligible for prizes you have to start working on your hack only at the event. Preparing you hack before would be considered as cheating and you would not be eligible for the prizes.

Can I sleep at the hackathon?

Yes, there will be some people sleeping. Just make sure you have everything you need to sleep comfortably. There will not be any beds. A lot of people will work through the whole night.
